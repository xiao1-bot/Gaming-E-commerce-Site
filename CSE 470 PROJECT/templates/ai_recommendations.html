How Our Enhanced AI Works

Our smart recommendation system analyzes:

  • Multi-genre analysis: Extracts all genre tags from your purchased games (e.g., "Action,Adventure,Sports")
  • Preference scoring: Identifies games that share any genre tags with your favorites
  • Review insights: Considers games you've rated 4+ stars to understand your taste
  • Diversity constraint: Limits to max 2 games per genre for variety
  • Smart matching: Recommends games with the highest genre overlap score
  • Fallback system: Shows popular games if you're new to the platform

Example: If you bought "Action,Adventure" games, we'll recommend max 2 Action games, max 2 Adventure games, and max 2 games with both tags for a diverse selection!
